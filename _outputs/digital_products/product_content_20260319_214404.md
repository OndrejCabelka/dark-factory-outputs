# Category 1: Listing Titles & SEO
### Prompts 1–9

---

**Prompt #1: Keyword-Rich Title Generator**
```
Act as an expert Etsy SEO specialist with deep knowledge of the Etsy search algorithm. 
I sell [YOUR PRODUCT TYPE] on Etsy targeting [YOUR TARGET CUSTOMER]. My product is 
[DESCRIBE YOUR SPECIFIC PRODUCT IN 2-3 SENTENCES]. 

Generate 5 different Etsy listing title options for this product. Each title must be 
exactly 140 characters or fewer, front-load the most important keywords in the first 
40 characters, use commas or pipes to separate keyword phrases naturally, and avoid 
keyword stuffing. For each title, explain which primary keyword you prioritized and 
why a buyer searching for this product would click on it. Also note the character 
count for each title.
```
*When to use: When creating a brand-new listing and you need multiple strong title options to choose from.*

---

**Prompt #2: All 13 Etsy Tags Generator**
```
Act as an Etsy SEO expert who specializes in tag strategy and long-tail keyword 
research. I am listing [YOUR PRODUCT TYPE] on Etsy. The product is [DESCRIBE YOUR 
PRODUCT — material, style, size, occasion, color, and who it's for]. My current 
listing title is: "[PASTE YOUR CURRENT TITLE HERE]."

Generate all 13 Etsy tags for this listing. Each tag must be 20 characters or fewer. 
Include a mix of: 2-3 broad category tags, 4-5 mid-tail descriptive tags, and 4-6 
long-tail niche tags that target specific buyer intent. Do not repeat any words from 
the title unnecessarily — tags should complement and expand the title's keyword 
coverage. Present the tags in a numbered list, and after each tag, write one sentence 
explaining the search behavior it targets.
```
*When to use: Every time you create or refresh a listing and need to maximize your tag coverage.*

---

**Prompt #3: Competitor Title Analyzer**
```
Act as an Etsy SEO strategist and competitive analysis expert. I am going to give you 
5 Etsy listing titles from top-ranking competitors in my niche. My product is 
[YOUR PRODUCT TYPE] and my target customer is [YOUR TARGET CUSTOMER].

Here are the 5 competitor titles:
1. [PASTE COMPETITOR TITLE 1]
2. [PASTE COMPETITOR TITLE 2]
3. [PASTE COMPETITOR TITLE 3]
4. [PASTE COMPETITOR TITLE 4]
5. [PASTE COMPETITOR TITLE 5]

Analyze these titles and identify: (1) the 5 most commonly used keywords across all 
titles, (2) keyword gaps — valuable terms that are underused or missing, (3) structural 
patterns they all follow, and (4) one weakness in their approach I can exploit. Then 
write me one superior title that incorporates the best keywords while differentiating 
from these competitors.
```
*When to use: Before launching a new product in a competitive niche to understand what's already ranking.*

```

[NUMBER OF MONTHS] months. My current title is: "[PASTE YOUR CURRENT TITLE]."

```

```
*When to use: When you want to scientifically improve a listing rather than guessing which title performs better.*

**Prompt #12: Feature-to-Benefit Converter**
```

"[PASTE YOUR CURRENT DESCRIPTION HERE]"
